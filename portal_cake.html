Portal Cake

This cake is real... or is it? Inspired by the infamous Portal game cake.

Ingredients:

  • 1 chocolate cake mix
  • Chocolate frosting
  • Cherries
  • Whipped cream (optional)
  • Chocolate sprinkles

Instructions:

  • Bake the cake mix according to box instructions
  • Let it cool and frost with chocolate frosting
  • Add sprinkles all over the top
  • Top with a cherry in the center (GLaDOS approved!)
