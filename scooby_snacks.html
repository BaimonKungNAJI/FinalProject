Scooby Snacks

Delicious, mysterious, and good enough to make Scooby and Shaggy run into haunted houses.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups peanut butter
  • 1 1/2 cups oats
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  • Dog bone cookie cutter (for fun shape!)

Instructions:

  • Mix all ingredients in a large bowl
  • Roll out dough and cut into shapes
